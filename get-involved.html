Volunteer Opportunities

Make a hands-on difference in children's lives through our diverse volunteer programs

Kitchen Operations

Assist in our certified kitchens with meal preparation, quality control, and packaging for school distribution.

  • Food preparation assistance
  • Quality assurance support
  • Packaging and logistics
  • Inventory management
Commitment: 4-6 hours/week
School Outreach

Work directly with schools to monitor meal distribution, engage with students, and ensure program effectiveness.

  • Meal distribution monitoring
  • Student interaction programs
  • Teacher collaboration
  • Impact documentation
Commitment: 3-4 hours/week
Event Management

Help organize fundraising events, awareness campaigns, and community engagement activities.

  • Event planning and coordination
  • Volunteer coordination
  • Vendor management
  • Post-event evaluation
Commitment: Project-based
Digital Marketing

Use your digital skills to amplify our message through social media, content creation, and online campaigns.

  • Social media management
  • Content creation
  • Photography/videography
  • Website maintenance
Commitment: Flexible
Data & Research

Support our impact measurement efforts through data collection, analysis, and research activities.

  • Survey data collection
  • Impact analysis
  • Report preparation
  • Database management
Commitment: 2-3 hours/week
Community Engagement

Build relationships with local communities, parents, and stakeholders to strengthen our program reach.

  • Community meetings
  • Parent engagement
  • Local partnerships
  • Awareness campaigns
Commitment: 4-5 hours/week

Corporate Partnership Opportunities

Partner with us through your organization's CSR initiatives and create large-scale impact

CSR Partnerships

Align your corporate social responsibility goals with our mission to eliminate classroom hunger. We offer customized partnership programs that match your company's values and objectives.

  • Dedicated program funding
  • Employee engagement opportunities
  • Impact reporting and metrics
  • Brand visibility and recognition

Employee Volunteering

Engage your workforce in meaningful volunteer activities that build team spirit while making a social impact. We design programs that fit your company's schedule and goals.

  • Team building activities
  • Skill-based volunteering
  • Leadership development
  • Flexible scheduling options

In-Kind Donations

Contribute goods, services, or equipment that help us operate more efficiently. From kitchen equipment to transportation services, every contribution matters.

  • Kitchen equipment and supplies
  • Transportation vehicles
  • Technology solutions
  • Professional services

Innovation Partnerships

Collaborate with us on innovative solutions to improve our operations, from technology implementations to sustainable practices and efficiency improvements.

  • Technology development
  • Process optimization
  • Sustainability initiatives
  • Research collaborations
